Portfolio

A collection of projects I have developed and am continuing to develop.

GalleryDB

January 2022 - March 2022

A website that serves as a one-stop shop for people of all backgrounds to learn more about artwork, artists, and museums. It is intuitive and easily navigable for users to acquire information about art, with the goal to address questions such as: What artwork is made by which artist? What artwork is in which museum? Where are museums located?



Shop Green

Awarded Qualtric’s Best Data Visualization Tool Winner of UT Women in Computer Science Hackathon

February 2022

A Google Chrome extension that allows users to track their shopping habits and find more sustainable options by calculating the sustainability scores of online products and reporting their environmental impact to users. Its extended website then visualizes the composition of items in their shopping cart using various graphic elements.



Ms. Entrepreneur

Awarded Challenge Winner of JP Morgan Chase + Girls Who Code Work Prep Buildathon

June 2021

A social media platform that allows users to connect with and empower one another, find upcoming and nearby events, explore different businesses that they can support, and develop a better sense on how to manage their own companies. This product hopes to champion female and non-binary entrepreneurs by providing networking and funding opportunities for their business ventures, placing an emphasis on investing in the founder.



Free to Help

Awarded 1st Place Overall and PwC’s Most Social Impact Winner of UT Women in Computer Science Hackathon

February 2020

A Google Chrome extension that displays nearby volunteering opportunities for users to merge with their Google Calendars. It has been further designed as an extended app where students, organizations, and schools can verify attendance at community service events.



YouServe

July 2018

A website that allows users to connect with one another and diverse organizations to find volunteering opportunities that align with their interests. Includes both a public forum and a private journal for users to record and share their experiences.
